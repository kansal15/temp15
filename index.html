IIRO Resources

Resources

**IIRO** resources include research publications, policy advocacy materials, and community-driven initiatives aimed at shaping India's digital future. They focus on internet governance, cybersecurity, digital rights, and emerging technologies. Through workshops, discussions, and collaborative projects, IIRF empowers individuals with knowledge and tools for impactful engagement. As a volunteer-driven initiative, it welcomes students, researchers, and policymakers to contribute and drive positive change in the digital ecosystem.
